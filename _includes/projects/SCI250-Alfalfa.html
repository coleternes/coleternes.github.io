Martian Alfalfa Experiment

SCI-250 Spring 2020

This was an experiment that examined the growth of Alfalfa within various soils: sand as a control, Earth soil, and Martian soil. Unfortunately, before our group could gather data, the Covid-19 Pandemic hit, and we were forced to work with simulated data provided by our professor. My job in this team was to write the R code that would analyze the data and produce understandable graphs which would be attached to our final poster presentation.
