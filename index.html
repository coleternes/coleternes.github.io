Personal Project:

3D Procedural Dungeon Generator

3D Procedural Dungeon Generator

This generator is the level designer for my current indie project: Cryptnosis. Generating the floor plan was strongly inspired by this video with minor adjustments made. The process goes as follows: 1. Randomly place rooms in a 3D environment without overlaps 2. Use the centers of the rooms as vertices to create a Delaunay Triangulation Graph 3. Determine the Minimum Spanning Tree to connect all rooms 4. Randomly keep some excess branches to add loops to the floor plan 5. Convert the vertices at the center of the room to anchor points for hallway connections 6. Pathfind hallways between two anchor points using the A* algorithm
